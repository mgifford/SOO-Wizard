# UK Digital Sustainability Policy Framework - SOO Example

## Overview
This example demonstrates how to use the SOO Wizard for UK Government procurement to evaluate Web Sustainability Guidelines and develop policy frameworks for legislative alignment within the UK government context.

## Scenario
**Client:** UK Government (Cabinet Office/GDS)  
**Project:** UK Digital Sustainability Policy Framework (UKDSPF)  
**Goal:** Evaluate Web Sustainability Guidelines implementation across UK government while ensuring compatibility with existing UK legislation and policy frameworks

## Key Features of This Example

### 1. **UK-Specific Legislative Integration**
- Addresses compatibility with Equality Act 2010, PSBAR 2018, Data Protection Act 2018
- Aligns with Government Digital Service standards and design system
- Considers Public Contracts Regulations 2015 for procurement frameworks

### 2. **Multi-Jurisdictional Scope**
- Engages 12+ government departments
- Includes 3 devolved administrations (Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland)
- Balances central coordination with regional implementation needs

### 3. **International Leadership Positioning**
- Creates replicable framework for other nations
- Contributes to international sustainability standards
- Published under Open Government License for global access

## Policy Challenge Areas

### Environmental vs. Accessibility Balance
```yaml
Constraint: "Must maintain full compliance with Public Sector Bodies Accessibility 
Regulations 2018 and WCAG 2.1 AA standards"

Objective: "Citizens experience no reduction in digital service accessibility or 
usability when sustainability measures are implemented"
```

### Procurement Framework Integration
- Update existing government procurement criteria
- Include sustainability measures without disrupting established processes
- Balance environmental goals with value-for-money requirements

### Digital Inclusion Considerations
- Impact on citizens with limited internet access
- Compatibility with older devices and constrained data allowances
- No negative effects on vulnerable populations

## Research and Policy Development Scope

### Legislative Analysis Requirements
1. **Equality Act 2010 Compatibility**
   - Reasonable adjustments for disabled users
   - Non-discrimination in digital service delivery
   - Accessibility vs. sustainability trade-offs

2. **Data Protection Act 2018 Alignment**
   - Privacy-preserving sustainability measurement
   - Consent and user data in carbon tracking
   - GDPR compliance with environmental monitoring

3. **GDS Standards Integration**
   - Service design principles compatibility
   - User research and testing requirements
   - Performance and accessibility standards

### Multi-Departmental Engagement Strategy
- **Central Departments:** Cabinet Office, Treasury, BEIS, Defra
- **Service Delivery:** HMRC, DWP, Home Office, DVLA
- **Devolved Administrations:** Scottish Government, Welsh Government, Northern Ireland Executive
- **Specialist Bodies:** Ofcom, ICO, Equality and Human Rights Commission
